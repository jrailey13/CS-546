My Travel Destinations

San Diego, CA

My family and I went on vacation to San Diego this past summer. It was one of the best vacations I've ever had, and I can't wait to go back at some point. The weather is amazing year round, the beaches are beautiful, the food is delicious, and so much more.

  1. Coronado Beach
  2. La Jolla Beach
  3. The Taco Stand
  4. The San Diego Zoo

Hilton Head, SC

Hilton Head Island is another great vacation spot. My family and I went on vacation there in the summer of 2021. The beaches are fantastic and there are so many attractions to visit. Just watch out for gators!

  1. Coligny Beach
  2. Sea Pines Forest Preserve
  3. Shelter Cove

Chicago, IL

The windy city! My family and I went on a trip to Chicago after a soccer tournament I had in Indianapolis. I'll be honest, I was not as excited for this trip because we usually take our vacations somewhere warm and near the beach. However, I was pleasantly surprised by Chicago and the many attractions it has to offer. Whether it's unique shops, breath-taking museums, or sports games...Chicago has many attractions that will make your trip memorable.

  1. The Art Institute of Chicago
  2. Millennium Park
  3. Museum of Science and Industry
  4. Navy Pier
