Coronado Beach Sunset Walk

July 25th, 2023 7:45pm

The fam and I just went for a walk along Coronado Beach during sunset. What a sight! The way the sun reflected off the water was breathtaking.

San Diego, CA

Hilton Head Bike Ride

June 25th, 2021 3:00pm

In Hilton Head, everyone rents bikes and bikes around the town. My family and I just went for a fun bike ride through Sea Pines Forest Preserve. The weather was a perfect 71 degrees and everyone had a good time. I did work up quite an appetite though. Hopefully we get some fresh seafood for dinner.

Hilton Head, SC

Chicago Baseball Game

June 30th, 2017 6:00pm

Just went to see the Chicago White Sox play the New York Yankees. We wanted to see the Cubs, but they weren't in town. The score was 0-0 for the whole game, so my family and I left during the 7th inning. Just our luck, I looked at the score on our way home and the game was 4-3! We missed all the action.

Chicago, IL
